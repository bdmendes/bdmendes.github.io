---
title: To proffer (how to accept fate)
---

and if things just stayed the same  
suspended in this inexplicable state  
where you and I are lame and distracted  
with no further will than to accept fate  

provided no wars may separate us  
we seek not to be fired by machines  
we're keen not to get hit by a bus  
though it is a pristine way to die  

then we can just lie here, cancel time  
hug each other dressed up in a beach  
for we strive for feelings of this kind  
feelings that put doubt out of reach  

to be honest, I think we solved life  
this is all it has the ability to offer  
to get to that, all by your side  
that is all I am able to proffer.
